The Game of the Century??

"Wow this is an Awesome Game" is an upcoming indie game that goes into the idea of anything you would do in a normal game is boring, and you are tasked with actually creating your own game to play. The developers take a really interesting approach, some call lazy, while others call genius. Keep your eye out for more info while we approach the release date.
